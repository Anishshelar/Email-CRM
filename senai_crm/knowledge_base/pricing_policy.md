# Pricing Policy

## Plans and Pricing

| Plan | Monthly Price | Annual Price | Seats |
|---|---|---|---|
| Starter | $49/month | $470/year (save ~20%) | Up to 3 users |
| Standard | $149/month | $1,430/year | Up to 15 users |
| Pro | $299/month | $2,870/year | Up to 50 users |
| Enterprise | Custom | Custom | Unlimited |

All prices are in USD. Annual billing is paid upfront.

## Non-Profit Discount
Registered non-profit organisations (501(c)(3) in the US, or equivalent international status) receive a **30% discount on the Standard plan**, reducing the monthly price from $149 to $104.30.

To apply:
1. Email billing@company.com with subject "Non-Profit Discount Request".
2. Attach proof of non-profit status (determination letter or equivalent).
3. The discount is applied to the next invoice after verification.

Non-profit discount applies to the Standard plan only. It does not stack with other discounts.

## Pro-Rata Billing
When a customer upgrades their plan mid-billing-cycle, the upgrade charge is calculated pro-rata:
- Charge = (remaining days in cycle / total days in cycle) × price difference between plans.
- Example: upgrading from Standard ($149) to Pro ($299) on day 15 of a 30-day cycle costs $75 for that cycle.
- Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle; no pro-rata credit is issued for downgrades (account credits are issued instead).

## Enterprise Custom Pricing
Accounts requiring more than 50 seats, custom SLAs, or dedicated infrastructure receive custom pricing. Contact sales@company.com.

Enterprise pricing factors:
- Number of seats and expected API volume.
- Data residency requirements (EU/APAC incur additional costs).
- Required SLA tier (standard 99.9% uptime is included; higher tiers are available).
- Professional services and onboarding support.
